Gold
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Gold (g) is an essential resource in The Pit. Similarly to XP, accumulating it is required to prestige. A player can view how much gold they currently have in the sidebar on the right side of their screen.
The following are a variety of ways players may obtain gold and increase their gold-earning rate:
  • Killing players with a bounty will award the killer with the bounty.
  • Placing in the top twenty players in major events will give a player several thousand gold.
  • For new players, minor events such as Giant Cake, King of the Ladder, Dragon Egg, and King of the Hill are great ways to obtain small amounts of gold.
  • Mystic sword enchants such as Moctezuma and Gold Boost were made to increase players' gold output.
  • Gold Boost is an upgrade purchasable at level 10. Each tier grants an additional +10% more gold from kills and ingots.
  • Conglomerate is a perk unlocked in the Renown Shop. Unlocked at prestige VIII, it removes the ability to earn XP, transmuting every XP earned into 0.20g.
  • The Highlander Megastreak grants the user +110% gold from all kills and the ability to earn one's bounty after dying. It is a Renown upgrade available at prestige VII.
  • Renown upgrades such as Renown Gold Boost, Celebrity, and Co-op Cat are extremely useful for increasing a player's gold output.
In order to prestige, a player needs to have grinded a certain amount of gold. This does not necessarily mean that the player needs to have that amount of gold in their inventory; the gold simply needs to have been earned at any point during the prestige.
Actions such as trading do not count towards the gold requirement.

Players are able to trade up to 50,000 gold per day. Additionally, gold can also be used to purchase shop items, perks, upgrades, and items from Auction events.

Trivia
Before The Pit 1.0.5, gold requirements were extremely high, requiring as much as 18,480,000g grinded to prestige. The gold requirements for all prestiges are now capped at 2,000,000g each.
