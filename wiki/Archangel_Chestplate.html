Archangel Chestplate
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
The Archangel Chestplateis an item in the Hypixel Pit. It is a mystic item that has the same defense as a diamond chestplate. It is always enchanted only with the Guardian mystic enchantment, which grants a 10% damage reduction.

 Archangel chestplates can be obtained on the Genesis map from the Angel NPC after obtaining 4000 Angel Faction Points. Trivia
The Guardian enchantment can be disabled by the Dark enchant Combo: Venom, reducing the Archangel Chestplate's functionality to that of an unenchanted diamond chestplate.
