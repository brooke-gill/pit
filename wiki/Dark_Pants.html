Dark Pants
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Dark Pants are special types of Fresh Pants designed to counter mysticism. They can be crafted for 5 Chunks of Vile and 2 renown at night if the user has at least one tier of the Prestige VI renown upgrade Heresy. Every extra tier of Heresy the player has reduces the vile cost of Dark Pants by 1.
Dark pants function differently from fresh pants. Upgrading dark pants to tier I requires 10,000 gold and Heresy I, but unlike typical mystic pants, it does not require Mysticism IX. Tier I Dark Pants will always be enchanted with Somber and have 5-20 lives. To upgrade to tier II, the player must have Mysticism IX, Heresy III, and 60,000g, at which point the pants will be enchanted with a random dark enchant.
Dark pants enchantments typically are very strong, but have a negative effect to the user to compensate. The list of Dark enchants is as follows:
  • Somber: You are immune to mystic enchantments. You cannot use mystic enchantments while wearing these pants.
  • Hedge Fund: Gain +6g per kill. If the target was wearing Tier I+ mystic pants, gain +66g instead. Lose 50g on death.
  • Needless Suffering: Respawn with 30 hearts of absorption. Cannot heal by any method. Must die once while wearing these pants to gain absorption.
  • Sanguisuge: Heal 1 hp on hit (2s cooldown). Heal 2 hp instead if the Vampire perk is equipped. The regeneration effect has no effect on the user.
  • Lycanthropy: At nighttime, gain +10% XP and gold on kill, permanent speed I and regeneration II. Lose these effects with day breaks and you cannot gain speed for the day after wearing these pants.
  • Grim Reaper: If a player dies within 10 blocks, deal 4 damage to them and earn that damage as an assist on the player's death. Earn -80% gold and XP.
  • Misery: If your target is wearing leather armor, deal +1 true damage to them on all your hits. Take 0.6 true damage on all hits received.
  • Spite: If your target is wearing leather armor, deal +20% damage to them. Take 5% more damage from players wearing leather armor.
  • Mind Assault: Deal -60% damage. Every player within 12 blocks wearing leather armor increases your damage by +1.5. Max of +16.
  • RARE! Combo: Venom: Every third hit poisons the target for 12 seconds and yourself for 24 seconds. While poisoned, players receive the Somber effect.
  • RARE! Golden Handcuffs: After claiming a bounty, 4x the bounty is shared between all players on the server. Requires 30 players in the server to activate.
  • RARE! Nostalgia: Take -10% damage. Diamond armor glows as if enchanted.
  • RARE! Heartripper: Every life removed from a mystic through killing while wearing these pants grants these pants +1 bloodshard. Earning 27 bloodshards grants 1 pair of fresh pants. Consume 2 lives of this item when generating fresh pants. When dark pants gain this enchant on upgrade, their max lives do not increase.
If Dark Pants have 100 lives or more, they are referred to as Evil Pants.

Trivia
Dark Pants used to be as strong as chainmail pants, this was changed to leather in early 2020.
The time period the Nostalgia enchantment refers to is the time before the 0.3.5 update, when Protection I diamond armor could be found in Care Packages.
