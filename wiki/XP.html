XP
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
XP is an essential resource in The Pit. Similarly to gold, accumulating it is required to prestige. A player's XP determines their level and prestige. Players can view the XP they need to level up in the sidebar and their lifetime XP at the Stats NPC.
The following are a variety of ways players may obtain XP and increase their XP-earning rate:
  • Earning kills and assists rewards XP. Using upgrades such as XP Boost and El Gato will increase the XP players earn from kills.
  • For new players, minor events such as Dragon Egg, Giant Cake, 2x Rewards, and King of the Hill are great ways to obtain small amounts of XP.
  • Killstreaks such as Second Gapple, Explicious, Ice Cube, and Apostle to RNGesus give the user XP.
  • Mystic sword enchants such as Sweaty and Combo: XP increase players' XP outputs.
  • XP is an upgrade purchasable at level 10. Each tier grants an additional +10% more XP from most sources.
  • Conglomerate is a perk unlocked in the Renown Shop. Unlocked at prestige VIII, it removes the ability to earn XP, transmuting every XP earned into 0.20g.
  • The Highlander Megastreak grants the user +110% gold from all kills and the ability to earn one's bounty after dying. It is a Renown upgrade available at prestige VII.
  • Renown upgrades such as Renown Gold Boost, Celebrity, and Co-op Cat are extremely useful for increasing a player's gold output.
In order to prestige, a player needs to have reached level 120. The amount of XP to reach level 120 depends on the prestige.

Trivia
Before The Pit 1.0.5, gold requirements were extremely high, requiring as much as 18,480,000g grinded to prestige. The gold requirements for all prestiges are now capped at 2,000,000g each.
