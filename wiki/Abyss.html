Abyss
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
The Abyss, or Corals map, is a map in the Hypixel Pit. With its bright, colorful structures, it is one of the most diverse and vibrant maps in the game. It was the third map to be released, published on August 19th, 2018. The Abyss map appears after the Castle map and before the Genesis map in the map rotation.
