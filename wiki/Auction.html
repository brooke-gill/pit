Auction
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Auction is a minor event in the Hypixel Pit. When the event starts, one randomly-generated item will be put up for auction. All players that were in the lobby when the auction started can bid on the item using gold. Spending gold on the auction bypasses the gold trade limit. If the player loses the auction and is in the lobby when it ends, their gold will be refunded.
Players are unable to auction items themselves; the only way for players to sell items is through trading.

There are many types of items that may appear in an Auction event:
  • Mystic Swords
  • Mystic Pants
  • Philosopher's Cactus
  • Chunks of Vile
  • Fresh Pants
  • Renown
  • Diamond Armor
  • Funky Feathers, rarely
  • Mystic Repair Kits, very rarely
Trivia
A player bidding seven million gold on a Mystic Sword.
Before prestiging, higher-prestige players occasionally bid absurdly large amounts of gold on auctions as they have nowhere else to spend their gold.
The Mystic Pants enchants Pit MBA, Fractional Reserve, and Paparazzi are considered "exclusive auction enchants" and cannot be obtained from enchanting.
Admins, extremely rarely, auction off vanilla Minecraft items for sale. Named "admin items" by the community, these typically are blocks or flowers and are very valuable.
The Auction event was added in The Pit 0.4 - Events Extravaganza. Two players were credited for its creation; one suggested the name "Shop Frenzy" for the event.
