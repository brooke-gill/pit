Armageddon Boots
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
The Armageddon Boots are a mystic item obtained on the Genesis map. It is a pair of red dyed leather boots enchanted with the Evil Within mystic enchantment, which allows the player to ignore other players' Somber enchantments. Unlike most mystic items, the Armageddon Boots cannot be repaired with Magnum Opus or enchanted in the Mystic Well. The boots have 66 lives.

 Armageddon Boots can be obtained on the Genesis map from the Demon NPC after obtaining 4,000 Demon faction points. Its counterpart, from the Angel NPC, is the Archangel Chestplate.

Trivia
Before The Pit 1.0.5, the Armageddon Boots had 3 lives. Because of a change in the update that made it much more difficult to retain mystic lives, the boots' lives were increased.
The Evil Within enchantment can ironically be disabled by the dark pants enchant Combo: Venom, reducing the boots' functionality to that of unenchanted leather boots.
