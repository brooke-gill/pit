Rage Pants
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Rage Pants are special types of Fresh Pants. They can be crafted for 17 Chunks of Vile if the user has the Prestige IX renown upgrade Pure Rage.
Rage pants function differently from fresh pants. When enchanted to tier 1, they will always have 4 or 9 lives and 1-2 tiers of a special Rage enchant. Once enchanted to tier 1, rage pants become functionally identical to typical mystic pants.
The list of Rage enchants is as follows:
  • Ace of Spades: Take 1/2/3 less damage from Combat Spades.
  • Really Toxic: Hits apply stacks of toxicity for 30 seconds. Toxicity stacks reduce the target's healing by 2% per stack. Max 10/15/20 stacks.
  • Heigh-Ho: Deal 1/2/3 more damage to players with Mirror.
  • New Deal: Gain immunity to the Billionaire enchant. Also take 0/4/8% less damage.
  • Do it like the French: Deal 1/2/3 more damage to players wearing Golden Helmets.
  • Combo: Breaching Charge: Every 4/3/2 hits removes Resistance from the target.
  • Sybil: You count as 2/3/4 players to enchants and perks (excluding Shark).
  • RARE! Think of the People: Players within 10 blocks gain immunity to Executioner and Combo: Perun's Wrath. They also take 0/10/20% less damage from swords with those enchants.
  • RARE! Regularity: If the damage of your attack deals less than 2.2/2.6/3 damage, automatically strike again in 0.1s. The second strike hits for 40/60/75% of the first hit's damage.
If the Rage enchant is upgraded to level 3, the pants gain the Malding prefix. If the pants have 8 total tokens, they gain the Manic prefix.

Trivia
On release, a RARE! rage enchant called Deal with the Devil was also released. It disabled the Devil Chicks enchant in an entire lobby. The enchantment was removed almost immediately.
If a pair of Rage Pants are upgraded to Malding or Manic, it is announced to the entire lobby.
