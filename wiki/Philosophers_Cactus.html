Philosopher's Cactus
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
The Philosopher's Cactus is an item used to create fresh pants. They can be obtained through auctions, trading, and Uberdrops.
Right-clicking the Philosopher's Cactus opens a menu that allows the user to select the color of pants they desire. Special pants, such as dark pants and aqua pants, cannot be obtained from the Philosopher's Cactus. Unlike the Pants Bundle, 64 Philosopher's Cacti can be stored in one inventory or ender chest slot, allowing the player to keep more items.

Trivia
Despite not being stated in the item's description, Philosopher's Cacti are kept on death.
To save time when typing, "Philosopher's Cactus" is commonly abbreviated as "philo" by the community.
