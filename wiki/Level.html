Level
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Levels are arguably the most quintessential aspect of The Pit. Leveling up unlocks new features, perks, upgrades, and commands. A player's XP determines what level they have.
New abilities are unlocked when a player levels up. For example, purchasing upgrades and perks is unlocked at level 10. A player's ender chest is unlocked permanently at level 15. Contracts can be started at level 30, trading is unlocked at level 60, viewing players with [/view] is unlocked at level 70, and prestiging is unlocked at level 120.

A player's level can be seen by the number that is inside of their brackets visible in the tab list and chat. In The Pit's tab list, players are sorted by their level, not prestige. A player's level determines the color of this number— starting at gray for levels 1 to 9, this color cycles through the rainbow in reverse order, changing color every ten levels. Additionally, the number becomes bold at level 60 and above.

The XP that a player needs to level up is determined by the player's level's base XP (see table above) multiplied by their prestiges's multiplier (found in the XP Leveling Table on the Prestige page), rounded up to the nearest XP. Trivia
A player is halfway through a prestige upon reaching level 94.
Despite 24 being numerically one-fifth of 120, level 24 is only approximately 1% of the way to level 120.
In the Prototype version of The Pit, the maximum achievable level was 100, requiring 1,000,000 XP (prestige IX today). This was changed in The Pit 0.2.
