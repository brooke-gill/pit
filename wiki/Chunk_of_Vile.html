Chunk of Vile
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Chunks of Vile, commonly referred to as "vile", are items in The Pit that are used to create special types of fresh pants. They can be earned in contracts with the Heresy Renown upgrade, completing Night Quests, and through auctions.

With the Heresy Renown upgrade unlocked at prestige VI, fresh dark pants can be crafted with 5 Chunks of Vile. This cost decreases by 1 for every level of Heresy the player has unlocked (minimum of 3). With the Pure Rage renown upgrade unlocked at prestige IX, Fresh Rage Pants can be crafted using 17 Chunk of Vile.

Due to the popularity of vile, it is commonly used as currency in trading because of the gold trading limits.
Chunks of Vile can also be used to repair mystic items. The megastreak Magnum Opus will consume Chunks of Vile to repair up to four lives on a mystic item. One Chunk of Vile will be expended for every life repaired, unless the item has had a Totally Legit Gem applied, in which case two will be consumed per life.

Trivia
The "Do Your Own Carpentry" Renown upgrade increases the chance to grant Chunk of Vile from contracts and grants players twice as much vile than usual for completing contracts.
