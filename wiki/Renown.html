Renown
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Renown is a special currency unlocked when the player prestiges for the first time.
Renown can be spent in the Renown Shop, where it can be spent to unlock Renown Upgrades, Renown Perks, Renown Shop Items, and Megastreak Bundles.
When prestiging, the player gains renown. The amount gained is based on the prestige the player is prestiging to.
  • Prestige 1 - 4: 10 renown
  • Prestige 5 - 9: 20 renown
  • Prestige 10 - 14: 30 renown
  • Prestige 15 - 19: 40 renown
  • Prestige 20 - 24: 50 renown
  • Prestige 25 - 29: ??? renown
  • Prestige 30 - 50: ??? renown
Renown can also be obtained from Major Events, the amount of which depends on how high the player placed during the event.
  • Team Deathmatch: 1 renown for scoring within the top 20, 2 renown for scoring within the top 3
  • Robbery: 1 renown for scoring within the top 20, 2 renown for scoring within the top 3
  • Rage Pit: 1 renown for scoring within the top 20, 2 renown for scoring within the top 3
  • Pizza: 1 renown for scoring within the top 20, 2 renown for scoring within the top 3
  • Beast: 1 renown for scoring within the top 20, 2 renown for scoring within the top 3
  • Spire: 1 renown for scoring within the top 20, 2 renown for scoring within the top 3, plus one for reaching floor 6 at least once
  • Blockhead: 1 renown for scoring within the top 15, 2 renown for scoring within the top 5
  • Squads: 1 renown for scoring within the top 7 squads, 2 renown for scoring within the top 3 squads
  • Raffle: 1 renown for depositing at least 27 tickets
